FAQ

Common questions

If you have any questions or concerns please email support@everwash.com or call (215) 618-8808.

You can update your credit card information on the Account Settings page by clicking the button labeled "Update Payment Info". The Account Settings page can be reached by clicking the gear icon from the bottom bar on ios devices, and from the left-hand side menu on android.

Right before you submit your payment, on the page with the "Submit Payment" button, scroll down and click the button labeled "Enter Code" to enter your promocode.

You can change your email address from the Account Settings page. Visit your Account Settings and scroll down to the section labeled "Profile", then type your new email address into the email field, and then click the "Save Changes" button. The Account Settings page can be reached by clicking the gear icon from the bottom bar on ios devices, and from the left-hand side menu on android.

Go to your QR code page in the app and click the button labeled "Add Another Car". You can also add an additional car by going to the "Locations" page to view the list of available wash locations, and selecting the location and wash plan you wish to add to your account.

You can read the complete terms of service here: https://app.everwash.com/terms.

You can view the upcoming billing date for your membership from the Account Settings page by scrolling to the bottom until you reach the "Membership" section. The Account Settings page can be reached by clicking the gear icon from the bottom bar on ios devices, and from the left-hand side menu on android.

The EverWash Membership is valid for up to one car wash per day, every day the wash is open for the designated vehicle only.
